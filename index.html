Paling Fences

Capped and uncapped paling in treated pine. Exposed posts, hidden posts, lapped or butted. Heights to suit boundary and council rules.

02

Picket Fences

Federation, flat-top, heritage and contemporary picket styles. Pre-primed and painted to your colour, or left raw for that weathered look.

03

Gates & Driveway Gates

Single, double and sliding gates in timber, steel or wrought iron. Hung straight, latched properly, braced so they stay that way.

04

Retaining Walls

About Homestead

A small family business that's been
building fences in Belmont for decades.

We're based on Jarrah Place in Belmont and we've spent the last few decades putting fences and gates into front yards, back yards, paddocks, pool surrounds and school grounds across Geelong, the Bellarine and the Surfcoast.

We're a small crew on purpose. The same hands measure up your job, dig the holes, hang the gates and clean up after themselves. No subbies. No flat-pack panels trucked in from Melbourne. Just custom builds, properly braced, set in concrete and finished to a standard we'd put on our own homes.

We're fully insured, Emu Wire preferred-fencer accredited, and we'll never quote you for work that doesn't need doing.
